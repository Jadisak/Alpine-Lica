About Us

Lica Club was founded on the principle that less is more. We believe in fostering a community centered around genuine interaction, shared values, and a focus on what truly matters. We are a collective of individuals who appreciate design, technology, and intentional living.

Our team is passionate about creating a platform that is not just a place to visit, but a community to belong to. We regularly host workshops, online meetups, and creative challenges that are designed to inspire and connect.
